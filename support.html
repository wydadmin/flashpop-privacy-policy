Common Questions

  • Microphone access: Flash Pop uses your microphone so you can answer trivia questions out loud. You can manage microphone permissions in your device settings.
  • Subscriptions: Subscriptions and purchases are managed through your Apple account. You can manage or cancel subscriptions in the App Store settings.
  • App not recognizing answers: Make sure microphone access is enabled, speak clearly, and check that your device volume and microphone are working properly.
  • Bug reports: Please include your device model, iOS version, app version, and a short description of what happened.
