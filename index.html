Flash Pop Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 27, 2026

Flash Pop ("Flash Pop," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use the Flash Pop mobile application and related services (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information you provide, such as email or sign-in credentials.
  • Gameplay data including scores, progress, categories played, and settings.
  • Microphone input used to power voice-answer gameplay features.
  • Device information such as device type, OS version, app version, and diagnostics.
  • Subscription and purchase metadata through Apple and RevenueCat.

2. Voice Data

Flash Pop may request microphone access so you can speak answers during gameplay. Voice input may be processed on-device or through third-party speech recognition providers solely to enable gameplay. We do not store raw voice recordings unless explicitly disclosed in-app or required for support, security, or legal compliance.

3. Subscriptions and Purchases

Payments are processed by Apple. We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions, entitlements, and purchase status. RevenueCat may process anonymous identifiers and transaction metadata necessary to provide these services.

4. How We Use Information

  • Operate and improve the app
  • Enable gameplay features
  • Save scores and progress
  • Manage premium access and subscriptions
  • Analyze performance and fix bugs
  • Prevent fraud or misuse

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers that help operate the Service, payment platforms, or where required by law.

6. Your Choices

  • Disable microphone access in device settings
  • Manage subscriptions in your Apple account settings
  • Delete the app at any time

7. Children’s Privacy

Flash Pop is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction).

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised date.
